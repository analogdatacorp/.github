## About Us

**Analog Data** is a Bengaluru-based technology company fusing the physical and digital worlds — from deeply embedded firmware to cloud-connected intelligence. We build production-grade IoT systems, edge AI pipelines, and learning platforms that help engineers ship real things.

> *"We convert raw analog signals into actionable cloud insights — and we teach others how to do the same."*
